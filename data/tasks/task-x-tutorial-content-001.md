## The Proven Formula (@bloggersarvesh)

**Format:**
1. **Hook:** Controversial claim ("X has a BIG PROBLEM")
2. **Tool:** Specific AI tool (Claude Cowork)
3. **Comments:** Detailed step-by-step instructions
4. **Value:** 100% actionable workflow

**Example:**
> "CHATGPT HAS A BIG PROBLEM. Claude can now do local SEO for you. ChatGPT just talks about it."
> 
> **Step 1:** "Claude Cowork can analyze your competition's weaknesses..."
> **Step 2:** "Claude Cowork can understand your business and competitors..."
> **Step 3:** etc...
